About me

The search of a life purpose cames me to realize that I needed soemthing more than dedicating myself to "working to sorvive", that I needed the life beatiful things and the simple moments of each day, that I needed dance, art. Each one of us feels that call, to excel, to grow, to be better, the key point is to find the specific area in which that desire becomes a purpose and, at the end, in something real.

This site is dedicated to exposing the transformation process since the beginning of my preparation in art development and the discovery of my love for performing arts, perhaps with errors and with the lack of technique and beauty of an experienced artist, but always wholeheartedly. At the same time, I try to show health and discipline tips that have helped me, as well as those little motivators that makes my life happier.
